our story

It all started with a simple dream: to bring happiness with every bite. Dessert Factory was born in South Jakarta with a mission to craft desserts that not only taste great but also create cherished memories for every occasion.

In our kitchen, every dough is prepared with premium ingredients, creativity, and love. Our pastry team ensures that every detail is perfect – from the soft textures, delightful toppings, to the mouth-watering aromas.

Whether it’s a birthday, wedding, or just a sweet gift for yourself, Dessert Factory is ready to be part of your special moments. We believe that desserts are more than just food – they tell stories, shared through flavors and memories with your loved ones.

Come visit us in South Jakarta and explore our special creations. With Dessert Factory, every moment can be sweeter and unforgettable.
